Personal Profile

A life with technology

My entire life has been influenced by technology. When I was 14 years old I had my first contact with a programming language, It was QBASIC which honestly, I did not like it. At that time I was getting further into something called Internet and in a world called HTML. In this language I did many simple pages with text and images. With the time I learned ASP language, thanks to it I got my first job in the year 2000 and I still using it today.

In 2005, my life changed dramatically, I was hired by a company as a Business Intelligence Consultant. In this role I learned many things, the most important was to develop for Microsoft SQL Server using DTS and DML/DDL. I was involved in a development group with which we did a system to captured data from different sources such as txt files, Excel and other database and then for to be processed with specified business rules to finally generate a big report. All this happened every month. This was done for one of the world's largest financial institutions.

In 2009, I was hired again in the first company where I worked. At this time, I developed modules written in Oracle PL/SQL, specializing me in how to tune DML and DDL structures, trying to get the most accurate result in the shortest time possible. I got involved in the development of a huge web app used to apply 360 evaluations to the company employees. This web app was written in ASP and PL/SQL, it took me 4 months to develop more than 50 pages and 200 database objects, fortunately I had the support of a great programmer, without him I could not have finished on time.

In 2012, I quit my job and moved to Australia ☺. I currently live in Melbourne and although I'm not closed to work as a Web Developer, however I would love to work in this country in my current passion as a Database Developer. I hope to get a company that gives me the opportunity to apply my knowledge and grow professionally in this wonderful place.
